About Me

Hey! I'm Stella Tom, a final year Computer Engineering Student at Don Bosco College of Engineering, Goa .

I have recently discovered my passion for product management and really excited to explore this field even more. During my undergrad studies(ongoing), I realised that it wasn't just Software Development that I wanted to do everyday in my life. It did not tick alot of boxes in my future everyday to-do lists. Solving Problems, coming up with creative ideas, challenging myself everyday,talking business,brainstorming(well that's my favourite cause the amount of ideas gotten in the process are insane),and get to think different everyday. Little did I know this is what Product Managers do and Bravo! I had found my Dream Career. The thought of being able to contribute in products from ideation to launch is really exciting and what I am really looking forward to.

The other areas of my interests include Data Science, Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, Management and Story-telling( I love weaving stories around ordinary-everyday stuff). I also enjoy engaging with various communities and mentor other students as well. Oh that reminds me, I am the Co-organiser of Women in Machine Learning and Data Science (WiMLDS) Goa community and one of the Creative Team Leads of Google Developers Group (GDG) Goa.
In my free time I love singing,painting and reading books,a #paulocoelho and #enidblyton fan.

Long story short; I am an aspiring Product Manager.
